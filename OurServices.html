Our Service

Qurx is a creative market startup aiming to be the one stop destination for all kinds of content creators looking for an extra hand in their creative labor and freelancers alike who are willing to take up projects to unleash their creativity. Our primary aim is to function as a managerial body for content creators who are looking to outsource their workflow, creating their very own personal pipeline for producing content.

Qrux’s core mission - Qrux’s crux - is to create a content creation oriented all inclusive agency, with a reliable work flow, and affordable prices. Our target audience are influencers and content creators who are growing, and have reached the point where they need to outsource work in order to grow, but simply hiring creative artists will break their budget.

The model is simple. We will create and manage a team of freelancers for the duration of your project, and work with said team directly. No more running after multiple freelancers various frealncers to ocmplete a single project. No more haggling for prices and and worrying about mediocre content. This is why we exist! You simply tell us what you want, and share any raw media you have! We will handle the rest.

We know what comes to mind when you read this. “Why don’t I hire freelancers directly?” Thats a great question, and in fact you should give it a try! Out source every part of your production process, everything except the actual creation of raw media, and then manage said freelance team until you have a final product. We guarantee you will loose more time then if you had done it yourself.

No amount of explanation can come close to the real thing, so we urge you to try us out for free, and then make your decision!
